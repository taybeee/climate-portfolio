## Overview

**Company:** Lumora Technologies B.V.
**Headquarters:** Amsterdam, Netherlands
**Sector:** Consumer Electronics (NACE C26 — Manufacture of computer, electronic and optical products)
**Revenue:** $2.83B (FY2023)
**Employees:** 14,200 globally
**Products:** Laptops, tablets, and peripherals (keyboards, monitors, docking stations)
**Markets:** Europe (48%), North America (31%), Asia-Pacific (21%)

Lumora is publicly listed on Euronext Amsterdam. As a EU-headquartered company above the CSRD threshold, it is subject to CSRD reporting obligations beginning FY2025. It has not yet set SBTi targets. It has a partial renewable energy procurement program covering three European facilities.

## Energy and Climate Context

Lumora signed the RE100 initiative in 2021 and has a stated goal of 100% renewable electricity by 2030. As of FY2023, renewable electricity covers F01, F02, and F03 via direct PPAs and green tariffs. All other facilities use grid electricity with no renewable procurement in place.

Lumora has no current SBTi commitment. Internal targets exist for Scope 1 and 2 only (net zero by 2040) but have not been independently validated. Scope 3 reporting is incomplete — categories 1, 4, 11, and 12 have been estimated for FY2023 but with varying data quality.

## Known Data Quality Issues (Pre-Audit)

The following issues are known to the sustainability team prior to the data audit:

1. F06 (Monterrey) — natural gas consumption data is missing for Q3 and Q4 2023 due to a metering system failure
2. F04 (Ho Chi Minh City) — electricity data is based on invoices denominated in VND; currency conversion and unit extraction have not been independently verified
3. F08 (Chicago Distribution) — facility was acquired mid-year (March 2023); only 9 months of data are available but have been annualized without disclosure
4. Scope 3 Category 11 — use of sold products emissions estimated using global average energy intensity figures, not product-specific measurements
5. Scope 3 Category 1 — purchased goods and services calculated using spend-based method only; no supplier-reported primary data
