# Sustainability & Climate Risk Portfolio

An AI-augmented sustainability and climate risk portfolio demonstrating end-to-end consulting workflows for a simulated client, Lumora Technologies B.V. — a consumer electronics manufacturer headquartered in Amsterdam. Built using Claude Code with a configured CLAUDE.md methodology spanning GHG accounting, SBTi target-setting, physical and transition risk assessment, double materiality, and IFRS S2/CSRD disclosure.

## Project Steps

| Step | Title | Status | Deliverables |
|------|-------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | AI-Augmented Workflow Setup | Complete | CLAUDE.md, workflow explainer |
| 2 | Data Audit, GHG Accounting & RAG Parser | Complete | Data quality memo, GHG inventory, RAG receipt parser |
| 3 | Physical Risk Assessment & Multi-Hazard Map | In Progress | Risk assessment report, interactive asset map |
| 4 | Transition Risk Assessment & Integrated Narrative | Planned | Integrated risk assessment |
| 5 | Double Materiality Assessment | Planned | Materiality matrix, IRO register |
| 6 | IFRS S2 / CSRD Disclosure Narrative | Planned | Full disclosure document |
